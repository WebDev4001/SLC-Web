About the Student Library Council

The Student Library Council (SLC) at Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT) is a dedicated body working to enhance the academic library experience for all students, faculty, and alumni. We act as a bridge between the student community and the library administration, advocating for student needs and contributing to the continuous improvement of library services.

Our mission is to foster a vibrant learning environment, promote responsible use of library resources, and organize initiatives that enrich the intellectual life of the university. From suggesting new acquisitions to organizing workshops and cultural events, SLC is committed to making the MMMUT Library a central hub for knowledge and innovation.
